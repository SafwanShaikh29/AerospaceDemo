Services

Drone Inspections

Visual inspections with high-resolution cameras and automated flight plans. Typical use cases: powerline inspection, solar farm assessment, wind turbine checks, and rooftop surveys.

LiDAR Surveys

Aerial LiDAR scanning produces accurate, densely sampled point clouds for topographic mapping, corridor surveys, and vegetation analysis.

Aviation & Surveillance

Fixed-wing and multirotor operations for long-duration missions, monitoring, and situational awareness for public safety and defense use cases.

Data Processing & Delivery

Turnkey data processing pipelines: orthomosaics, point clouds, classified deliverables, and GIS-ready products. Secure delivery options and analytics dashboards available.

Industries We Serve

Energy & Utilities

Inspection and monitoring of power lines, substations, pipelines and renewable assets to improve safety and uptime.

Construction & Infrastructure

Progress monitoring, earthworks measurement, and site health monitoring using aerial imagery and LiDAR.

Public Safety & Emergency Services

Rapid aerial assessment for incident response, search & rescue, and scene mapping.

Agriculture

Crop health mapping, NDVI analytics, and precision agriculture support.

Mining & Survey

Stockpile measurement, volumetrics, and site safety monitoring.

Telecom & Utilities

Tower inspections and right-of-way assessments to reduce maintenance costs.

Training & Certification

Professional training programs for commercial drone pilots. Courses include operations theory, flight safety, regulations, and practical flight time. Available as classroom, onsite corporate, or online modules.
